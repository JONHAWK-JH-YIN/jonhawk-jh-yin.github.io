Economic History and AI-Assisted Data Engineering

Details

Research Assistant | Project led by Prof. Pei Gao and Prof. Yu-Hsiang Lei
June 2025 – Present
Project: The Persistent Impact of Historical Conflicts in China

Keywords: economic history; historical conflicts; local gazetteers; structured data engineering; LLM-based extraction

I serve as the primary undergraduate coordinator at the University of Macau for an economic history project on the persistent impact of historical conflicts in China. The project involves constructing village-level historical datasets from gazetteers and local chronicles.

Main Contributions:
• Helped build a standardized 33-variable village-level coding framework.
• Identified and coded variables from historical gazetteers and local chronicles, including population, religious institutions, infrastructure, migration, education, and institutional features.
• Designed and refined large language model prompts for structured information extraction from raw TXT sources.
• Contributed to a scalable TXT–JSON–CSV data transformation pipeline.
• Modified Python scripts for splitting digitized PDF books and supported automated PDF segmentation for batch processing and validation.

Regional Collaborative Governance and Social Network Analysis

Details

Research Assistant | Prof. Shuwen Zhang, University of Macau
Summer 2026

Keywords: public administration; collaborative governance; regional governance; social network analysis; intercity cooperation
